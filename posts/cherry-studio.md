### Financial Projections

For a more detailed financial model, businesses can estimate the ROI using the following formula:

\[ ROI = \frac{(Net Profit from Investment - Cost of Investment)}{Cost of Investment} \times 100\% \]

Where the **Net Profit from Investment** includes quantified benefits like time savings, labor cost reductions, and potential revenue increases over a standardized period, while the **Cost of Investment** reflects any expenses associated with adopting and implementing Cherry Studio.
