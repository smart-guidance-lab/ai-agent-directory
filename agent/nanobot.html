### 2. High-Value Business Use Cases

**Use Case 1: Medical Applications**
- **Description:** Nanobots can be designed to target and deliver drugs directly to cancer cells, minimizing damage to healthy tissues and improving treatment efficacy.
- **Why It Matters:** This approach can lower side effects of treatments, enhance patient recovery times, and potentially customize therapies for individual needs.

**Use Case 2: Environmental Remediation**
- **Description:** Nanobots can be deployed to identify and neutralize pollutants in water systems or soils, breaking down contaminants at a molecular level.
- **Why It Matters:** This can lead to more effective clean-up processes for oil spills, heavy metal contaminants, and other environmental hazards, leading to sustainable practices and compliance with regulations.

**Use Case 3: Manufacturing Precision**
- **Description:** In manufacturing, nanobots can be utilized for assembling products at a microscopic level, improving precision and reducing waste.
- **Why It Matters:** This can significantly enhance product quality, reduce costs, and enable the creation of materials with novel properties, such as super-strong composites or smart materials.
