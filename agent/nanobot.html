## 2. High-Value Business Use Cases

### Use Case 1: Targeted Drug Delivery
Nanobots can be engineered to deliver drugs directly to specific cells or tissues in the body. This precision can minimize side effects, increase treatment efficacy, and reduce the overall dose of medication needed.

### Use Case 2: Environmental Remediation
Nanobots can be deployed to clean up pollutants or hazardous materials in environments such as soil, water, and air. They can identify and neutralize toxins efficiently, offering an innovative solution to environmental challenges.

### Use Case 3: Advanced Manufacturing
In manufacturing, nanobots can facilitate the creation of nanoscale components and materials, allowing for higher precision in production, enhanced material properties, and reduced waste, leading to more sustainable manufacturing practices.
